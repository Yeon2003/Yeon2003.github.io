Battle Angel:Alita is a comic book set against the background of my usual favorite sci-fi style,
which encouraged me to draw a cyberpunk/sci-fi style of drawing.
Especially, I really liked the drawing style of Yukito kishiro, who drew Battle Angel:Alita.
The complex mechanics and cyborg structures excite me.

The series is set in the post-apocalyptic future and focuses on Alita
("Gally" in the original Japanese version, and several other countries),
a female cyborg who has lost all memories and is found in a junkyard by a cybernetics doctor who rebuilds and takes care of her.
She discovers that there is one thing she remembers, the legendary cyborg martial art Panzer Kunst,
which leads to her becoming a Hunter Warrior, or bounty hunter.
The story traces Alita's attempts to rediscover her past and the characters whose lives she impacts on her journey.
The manga series is continued in Battle Angel Alita: Last Order and Battle Angel Alita: Mars Chronicle.
